my internet philosophy

take the space

the internet is yours. wander, explore, make mistakes, and leave traces. don’t get lost in the noise or swallowed by the algorithms

you’re the one who decides what matters, how you spend your attention, what you create

try things, break things, share things, and see what happens. stay curious, stay moving, stay alive online

fuck distraction fuck the algorithm fuck the dead internet sludge fuck personal brands pretending to have souls fuck the coaches, the hustlers, the matrix, the stage play of society fuck all of it

because at the end you can just do things

play your own way

curiosity > productivity

experiment first, explain later

break the rules, follow your own path, see what sticks

your browser is your playground. chaos is part of the fun. explore weird corners, leave traces, make something yours

you can carve your own corner too. make a personal website. make it messy, make it live, make it real

attention is optional, creation is mandatory. you can just do things

knowledge sharing

the internet grows when people share. gatekeeping does no one any favors. document your work, share tools, teach others

the strange and broken corners hold the most interesting secrets. explore them, celebrate them, make them visible
